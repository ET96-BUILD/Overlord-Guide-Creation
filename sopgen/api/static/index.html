    Publish to Overlord with Cowork

    1. Extract the downloaded zip to a folder.
    2. Open Cowork (the desktop app) and grant it access to that folder.
    3. Sign into Overlord, Finance, or whichever iFixit subdomain you're publishing to in Chrome.
    4. Open a new guide form at <subdomain>.ifixit.com/Guide/new/Topic/Root and have that tab visible.
    5. Click Copy Cowork prompt below, then paste it as your message in Cowork. Cowork creates the guide skeleton, fills in all steps and substeps, and applies bullet styling.
    6. After Cowork finishes, drag the PNG images from your extracted folder into each step's image dropzone manually.
    7. Review the guide. Privacy is set to Private by default — share the URL with whoever needs to review.
